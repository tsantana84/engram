Who is the developer audience?

This shapes depth, tone, and what to emphasize

A

Your team at Cint

Developers who already know the codebase. Focus: how to install, what sync does, trust the new memory quality features.

B

External developers / open source adopters

Strangers to the project. Focus: why use this, install guide, what they get out of the box, privacy/trust.

C

Both — general developer audience

Mixed. Cover install + internals + team sync. Works as onboarding AND public pitch.
