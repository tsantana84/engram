Scaling

Limits & scaling

Two separate limits — storage and context injection. They hit at very different scales.

Storage — not your bottleneck

LayerLimit
SQLite (local)Billions of rows. Practically unlimited.
Supabase free tier500MB ≈ ~250k observations
Supabase paidScales to TB
Chroma (vector)Memory-bound. Slow rebuild at 50k+ obs

Context injection — the real limit

How it works
Every session start, engram injects past observations into Claude's context window. That window is finite.
Current scale
50 observations ≈ 16K tokens injected. At that rate, 300–400 obs consumes a 100K context window entirely.
The knob
CLAUDE_MEM_OBSERVATION_LIMIT
in ~/.engram/settings.json — tune as corpus grows
ℹ️
Compression (session summaries) already stretches the injection budget. Storage will never be the problem — context window is the binding constraint.
